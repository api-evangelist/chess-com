Wesley So maintained his lead at the 2026 Norway Chess tournament after surviving a challenging match against World Champion Gukesh Dommaraju in classical play, though Gukesh claimed victory in the subsequent armageddon game. Bibisara Assaubayeva extended her advantage in the women's competition to 2.5 points following a decisive win over Zhu Jiner.
