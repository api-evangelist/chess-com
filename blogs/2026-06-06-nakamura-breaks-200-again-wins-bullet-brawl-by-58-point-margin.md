GM Hikaru Nakamura dominated the June 6, 2026 Bullet Brawl competition, racing to a 201-point victory with no rivals in sight and finishing 58 points ahead of second-place Oleksandr Bortnyk. Nakamura compiled 56 wins, two draws, and lost just eight games in two hours, including a 14-game winning streak and a remarkable 22-out-of-23 scoring spurt in the final 30 minutes of the event.
