GM Oleksandr Bortnyk achieved a historic victory on June 5, 2026, becoming the first player to win nine Freestyle Fridays and securing his third championship title in the 2026 season. Starting with a perfect 9/9 record, he maintained his lead through the tournament's conclusion with a final score of 9.5 points and claimed the $400 first prize.
