Quantitative trading firm Jane Street emerged victorious in the North American Corporate Chess League's 13th season, defeating JPMorgan Chase in an exciting final match. The NACCL represents the world's largest corporate chess competition, featuring numerous major companies and smaller enterprises competing for the title.
